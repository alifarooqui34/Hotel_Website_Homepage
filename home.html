Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

Check-in is from 03:00 PM, and check-out is until 11:00 AM. You may request early check-in or late check-out during booking, subject to availability. Guests checking in or out before or after the designated periods may be charged an additional fee. The hotel offers luggage storage to guests for before check-in and after check-out. The front desk is always open, day or night.

For those who wish to drive their own cars, Hotel A Tainan has a car park on site, as well as additional parking nearby if the first car park is full. If you're looking for an easy way to get around Tainan with private transportation, the hotel can arrange taxi service.

Wake up to a great buffet breakfast at Hotel A Tainan. Western breakfast is available for guests. There's an additional charge of 317 TWD for adults and 200 TWD for children if not included in the room rate. You may save on breakfast costs with Agoda by booking a room with breakfast, which is often included for less than buying it at the hotel.

On-site venues and convenient event services make Hotel A Tainan an ideal choice for hosting events such as corporate events and meetings. The venue available at the hotel can accommodate up to 30 people. An extensive range of facilities and services including projector or LED display, free Wi-Fi and meeting stationery is available to help your business meeting or event a successful one.
